About Me

Hello! I’m Li-Sheng Zhuang (莊立勝), currently a Research Assistant at the Institute of Statistical Science (ISS), Academia Sinica, working in Prof. Yen-Tsung Huang’s research group. I received my M.S. in Statistics and Data Science from National Tsing Hua University and hold a bachelor’s degree in Applied Mathematics with a double major in Finance from National Chung Hsing University.

My current work focuses on mediation analysis, with an emphasis on machine learning and nonparametric regression methods. From a methodological perspective, I am particularly interested in nonparametric approaches. Previously, my master’s thesis investigated causal mediation analysis under a non-Markov illness-death model, incorporating kernel density estimation method.

Project Experience

  • Thesis - Nonparametric Mediation Analysis of Non-Markov Illness-Death Model (2025): Used causal mediation analysis to explore applications in non-Markov illness-death data. [Link]
  • Fourier-Based Stock Price Modeling (2024): Applied Fourier Transform to denoise log returns in stock price data, improving prediction accuracy in a time series forecasting project. [Link]
  • A General Paired t-test under Non-i.i.d. Assumptions (2024): Applied the Lyapunov Central Limit Theorem and k-Nearest Neighbors to extend the classical paired t-test framework. [Link]
  • Futures and Options Pair Trading Bot (2023): Developed a pair trading bot for futures and options trading. [Link]
  • Credit Card Customer Loyalty Prediction (2021): Applied machine learning models to predict credit card customer loyalty. [Link]

Programming Languages

I am familiar with R, Python, and Matlab, and have hands-on experience using these tools for data analysis, model development, and statistical computing.
